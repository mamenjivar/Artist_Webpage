José; Ramón DeJesus (born October 18, 1978 in Manhattan, New York) known Artistically as Nomar (DeProdigy). He is a singer and songwriter of parents from Puerto Rican descent. At the age of 11 years, he moved with his family from New York to San Germán, Puerto Rico, where he lived until he enlisted in the United States Army in July 1998. His military career has taken him to different places in the world and he continues to serve as an active soldier while beginning his career as a singer.

From an early age he showed interest in music. While attending school at Laura Mercado de Rosario, Puerto Rico, he competed and won the contest of “buscando estrellas” in the television show “A Toda Máquina” in which he composed the song “Enamorado” and “No sé qué voy hacer”. He was part of a music group from Mayagüez, Puerto Rico called “fragmento 6” alongside Eliot “El Mago D Oz”. During his active duty service in the Army, José Ramón DeJesus auditioned and was accepted for “USAEUR Choir” in Germany, but due to responsibilities and military missions he could not be part of the prestigious choir.

His artistic career began with his first single "Enamorado" under the production of Eliot “El Mago D Oz”. Nomar's long awaited CD will feature a repertoire of varied music, from Pop music to ballads, reggae, danza and R & B. The CD will include songs in collaboration with Eliot “El Mago D Oz”, “Sheeno el Sensei” from Colombia, among others. The singer, Nomar, continues to explore his limits and apparently so far nothing stops him.

His single “Enamorado” is now on iTunes, CD Baby, Amazon, Google music, XBOX Music, among others. The video of “Enamorado” can be found on YouTube.

Nomar‘s music is currently playing on the radio in Columbus, Georgia at Unidos 107.7, the internet and the world at www.unidos1077.com, with three songs under the production of Eliot “El Mago D Oz”, his single “Enamorado” plus two more songs, “Angel” and “Para Enamorarte” which are part of his upcoming CD “Quimica Esencial”.
